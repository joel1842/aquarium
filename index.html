These are my fish!

This website is an online version of my real fishtank. I coded my own fish tank into JavaScript, so now my fish have been immortalized in zeros and ones. Here are some facts about, and pictures of, my fish!

Siamese Algea Eater

My algea eater spends most of his time hiding, occasionally appearing to do some housecleaning.

Gold Gourami

Gold Gourami's are great fish and have their own funny little personalities.

Pearl Gourami

Pearl Gourami's are a more shy and illusive. They tend to spend their days hiding in the plants!

Neon Tetra

The neon tetra naturally inhabits murky water, leading to its bright colors so they can find eachother!
